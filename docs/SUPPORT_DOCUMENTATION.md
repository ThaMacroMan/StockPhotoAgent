## How It Works

Send a search query → AI finds relevant photos from Pexels → Get 8-12 curated photos with attribution info.

**Example queries:**

- "modern coffee shop with cozy atmosphere"
- "tech startup office with diverse team"

## FAQ

**Are photos free?** Yes, all from Pexels. Free for commercial use with attribution.

**Do you save my queries?** No. Processed and discarded immediately.

**What about OpenAI?** Queries go to OpenAI for AI analysis. See their privacy policy.

**Attribution required?** Yes. Credit the photographer and Pexels.

## Attribution Format

```
Photo by [Photographer Name](profile-url) from Pexels
```

## Troubleshooting

- **Slow?** Check internet, try refreshing
- **Errors?** Ensure query is 5+ characters, try rephrasing
- **Still stuck?** Contact us
